Welcome to NakedBacteria

Bacteria do a lot with a little.

NakedBacteria is a project in R that makes it possible to see where genes reside on bacterial chromosomes and compare gene content as well as gene synteny (the physical co-localization of genetic loci on the same chromosome within an individual or species) across related species.
